# Time Capsule ✉️⏳

Send messages to the future – yours or someone else's.

## What is this?

Time Capsule is a **simple and beautiful web application** that lets you:

- Write a personal message
- Choose an exact date & time in the future
- Enter an email address
- "Seal" the message → it will be automatically sent to the chosen email address on the exact date you picked

Perfect for:
- Messages to your future self
- Surprising someone years later
- Long-term promises / reminders
- Romantic future letters
- New Year's resolutions you want to read later
- Life advice for your children / younger self

## How it works

1. You write your message  
2. You pick a future date & time  
3. You enter the email address that should receive it  
4. You click **"Seal & Send"**  
→ Your message is safely stored  
→ On the chosen date & time the recipient gets the email with your message

## Features

- Beautiful, clean, modern design  
- Works on mobile & desktop  
- No login / account needed  
- Messages are sent automatically at the exact time you choose  
- Very simple & emotional way to create something meaningful

## Technologies 

- HTML + CSS + Vanilla JavaScript  
- Google Apps Script (for storing & sending emails in the future)

## Want to use it yourself?

Feel free to fork the project and host it anywhere!  
(You will need to set up your own Google Apps Script endpoint for storing & sending)
